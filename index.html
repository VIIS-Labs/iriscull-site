Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

IrisCull focuses on one thing — making photo decisions fast. No editing tools, no cloud sync, no complexity.

⚙

Two Review Modes

Review Mode for rapid decisions with auto-advance. Inspect Mode for browsing filtered subsets without changing your choices.

🔍

Full EXIF Metadata

Camera, lens, exposure, ISO, focal length, GPS coordinates, file dimensions — all visible in a dedicated panel.

📦

Export & Trash

Export kept photos or favorites as copies or aliases. Batch move rejected photos to Trash with one click.

💾

Session Persistence

Your progress saves automatically. Close the app, come back later, and pick up right where you left off.

⭐

Preference Ratings

Rate photos 1–3 independent from keep/reject decisions. Find your favorites across any category.

📷

Wide Format Support

JPG, PNG, HEIC, TIFF, WEBP, and more. Works with whatever your camera shoots.
