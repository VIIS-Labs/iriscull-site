Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

IrisCull is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely on your local machine and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data externally.

Data Collection

IrisCull collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or web tracking

Photo Access

IrisCull accesses photos only from folders you explicitly select through the system file picker. Your photos are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded, copied to external servers, or shared with any third party.

Local Storage

Session data (your review decisions, ratings, and progress) is stored locally on your Mac in:

~/Library/Application Support/IrisCull/sessions/

Theme preferences are stored in standard macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Network Usage

IrisCull uses the network only for Apple's StoreKit framework to process in-app purchases and verify transaction receipts through the App Store. No other network requests are made.

Third-Party Services

IrisCull does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

IrisCull does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our support page.
