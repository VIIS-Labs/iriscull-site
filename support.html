Frequently Asked Questions

What photo formats are supported?

IrisCull supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, HEIC, HEIF, TIFF, WEBP, BMP, and GIF.

Where is my session data stored?

Session data is saved locally at ~/Library/Application Support/IrisCull/sessions/. It persists across app launches so you can resume any review.

Does IrisCull modify my original photos?

No. IrisCull never modifies, moves, or deletes your original files during review. Export creates copies or aliases, and Trash requires explicit confirmation.

How do I restore purchased themes?

Open the theme picker in the sidebar, select a locked theme, and tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall sheet. This will restore any themes previously purchased with your Apple ID.

What's the difference between Review and Inspect mode?

Review Mode (orange banner): Arrow keys make decisions and auto-advance to the next photo. Inspect Mode (green banner): Arrow keys navigate without changing decisions, useful for browsing a filtered subset.

Does IrisCull require an internet connection?

No. All core features work fully offline. Network is only used for App Store purchase/restore flows.
